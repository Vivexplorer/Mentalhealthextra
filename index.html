About Vivaan and Ved

Vivaan Shah and Ved Nakum are two inspiring students based out of Troy, Michigan. Vivaan is currently in seventh grade, while Ved is in eighth grade, both attending Baker Middle School. These young individuals share an extensive passion for mental health and strive to make a positive impact on their community.

Mental Health Advocacy

Vivaan and Ved firmly believe in the importance of mental health and are dedicated to spreading awareness and providing support. They actively participate in school programs and initiatives that focus on promoting mental well-being. Through presentations, workshops, and discussions, they aim to educate their peers and teachers about the significance of mental health and the resources available for those in need.
